Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Introduction

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was one of the most respected scientists and presidents of India. He played a major role in India's missile and space programs.

He was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. His full name was Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam.

He became the 11th President of India and inspired millions of students through his speeches and books.

Dr. Kalam believed in hard work, education and innovation. He always encouraged young people to dream big.

He received many awards including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
